How to Prepare Nkwobi Delicacy

This is the method to prepare Nkwobi Delicacy in harmony.

Ingredients

  • Cow leg
  • Palm oil
  • Utazi leaf
  • Potash
  • Maggi
  • Fresh pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil your cowleg in a pressure pot (if you have one)it cooks faster with only water
    till it is soft and cooked.
  2. Then pour palm oil into a mortar, add your grinded potash (if it is not grinded,
    you can soak it in water, then use the water).
  3. Mix until it is thick then add grinded fresh pepper, crayfish, maggi, salt.
  4. Mix all and taste the sauce to make sure there is enough salt and pepper to your taste.
  5. Then add the well cooked soft cowleg, mix with the sauce then add utazi leaf
